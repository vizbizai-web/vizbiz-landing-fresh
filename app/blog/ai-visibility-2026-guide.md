## Why Traditional SEO Isn't Enough

For 20 years, dealerships focused on:
- Keywords on websites
- Google Ads campaigns  
- Review generation

That worked when humans browsed search results.

But AI doesn't browse. **It synthesizes.**

When ChatGPT recommends a dealership, it doesn't show a list of 10 options. It gives **one answer** based on what it "knows" about local dealers.

**The question is: does it know about you?**

---

## The New Visibility Game

AI systems learn about businesses from:

1. **Citations** - Mentions across the web (directories, articles, press)
2. **Reviews** - Sentiment and volume across platforms
3. **Content** - Blog posts, videos, thought leadership
4. **Consistency** - Same name, address, phone everywhere
5. **Authority** - Backlinks from trusted sources

**Most dealerships have:**
- ❌ Inconsistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone)
- ❌ Reviews only on Google
- ❌ No blog content
- ❌ Zero citations beyond basic listings
- ❌ No AI-specific optimization

**Result:** Invisible to AI search.
